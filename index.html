About Us

Our company is considered one of the leading companies in law and legal services, as we cover all parts of Iraq and represent our clients, individuals or companies, in the federal and local courts of various degrees. Over more than 15 years of experience, we have won the largest cases in various types of criminal courts, labor courts, administrative corruption and first instance courts. This resulted in gaining the largest international companies in various specializations such as oil, medical and security companies and large commercial contracting. As an integrated legal team, we strive to provide the best legal services that preserves the rights and interests of clients. Our company contains highly qualified and highly experienced lawyers, supported by an efficient management system to provide high quality services to our clients.
